FAQs

You have questions? We have the answers!

How can I change my order?

To change your order, simply log into your customer account and navigate to the "Orders" section. Here, you can select and edit your order as needed. Please note that changes can only be made until the order begins processing, so it's advisable to make any modifications as soon as possible.

How can I cancel my order?

Go to "Orders" in your customer account and use the comment field to cancel items that have not yet been processed.

If the order has already been processed and shipped, you can either refuse to accept it or return the package, notifying us on the return shipping page.

Where can I pick up my order?

You can pick up your order at the KulturKaufhaus ServiceCenter, which is conveniently located on the first floor adjacent to the cash desks. This allows for an easy and efficient collection of your items.

What do I need to pick up my order?

If you have reserved your product online and want to pay for it on pickup, we only need your name and the shopping basket number

If you have already paid online and just want to pick up your order, bring an ID.

I have forgotten my password. What can I do?

If you have forgotten your password, click "My account" and select "Forgotten your password?". Enter your email address to receive a link for creating a new password.

Why isn’t my password being accepted?

To ensure your password is safe, there are guidelines; it must contain at least 6 characters and include at least one number or a special character.

My eBook purchase was not completed.

To buy an eBook you must use a German payment method or account. You must be logged into your customer account to buy eBooks. You cannot buy eBooks as a guest.

I can't open my eBook.

Open your PocketbookCloud with the Pocketbook App or in your browser. You should be able to open and read your eBook there.

Is my dog allowed in the KulturKaufhaus?

At KulturKaufhaus, both two-legged and four-legged visitors are welcome. Dogs are allowed inside, provided they are kept on a leash at all times. This policy ensures a comfortable and safe experience for all our patrons.

How can I buy a gift card?

To buy a gift card, first select the type of gift card you want from the retailer's website or store. Then, choose the amount you wish to add to the card. After selecting the amount, add the gift card to your shopping basket. Finally, proceed to checkout to complete your purchase, providing any necessary payment and delivery details.

A product is defective. What now?

We apologize if any item that we have delivered is faulty or damaged. Returning the product is simple: Go to the page return shipping, where you will find all the information you need.

If you are nearby, come to the service center where the amount will be refunded.

What are the shipment options?

Within Germany: Shipment with DHL and Deutsche Post is free of charge. The delivery usually takes 1-2 business days. Deliveries abroad: With DHL. Shipment costs are listed here.
